**Capacity estimation**:
    It refers to the **process of predicting how much demand your system can handle**. This is crucial for ensuring smooth user experience, effective growth planning, and cost efficiency.

Key Metrics for Capacity Estimation:
    1. Daily Active Users (DAU)
    2. Queries Per Second (QPS)
    3. Storage Requirements
    4. Response Time
    5. Error Rates - percentage of failed requests.
    6. Concurrent Users
